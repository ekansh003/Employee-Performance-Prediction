About the Project

Employees are the most valuable asset of any organization. Their productivity directly impacts operational efficiency and business growth. Companies that monitor and enhance employee performance gain a competitive advantage through improved outcomes.

This project utilizes machine learning to predict the productivity level of employees based on various measurable parameters such as overtime, incentive, team size, idle time, and more.

With data-driven insights, organizations can optimize task distribution, support low-performing teams, and reward high achievers.
